About Our Firm

Kansas City’s Medical Malpractice & Catastrophic Injury Law Firm

Resolving serious medical malpractice, catastrophic personal injury & wrongful death cases since 1986.

With little warning, your life and the lives of your loved ones may drastically change due to the negligent actions of another. Your health, your ability to earn a living, and your ability to enjoy a normal life may all be seriously harmed — sometimes permanently.

At Dempsey Kingsland Osteen, we understand the crises that arise from serious injuries and deaths caused by negligence. Those who cause permanent harm through negligent action or inaction should be held accountable. Founded in 1986, our firm has maintained a commitment to excellence in representing the seriously injured and their families.
